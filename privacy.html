Introduction

WasteTrack ("we", "our", "us") provides an app and website to help users log food waste, view environmental impact, earn EcoCredits, and connect with donation or recycling partners. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email (from Google sign-in) and any profile information you provide.
  • Usage & App Data: waste logs, credits balance, device metadata, and optional location data.
  • Photos & Media: photos you upload for verification (stored temporarily).

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the service (accounts, logs, dashboards).
  • Calculate environmental impact and manage EcoCredits.
  • Enable features like NGO pickups and coupon redemption.
  • Analyze and improve services.
  • Send service updates or support messages.

Legal Basis & Consent

By using the app, you consent to the collection and use of your information. We’ll request explicit consent for things like location or camera access.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only when:

  • Enabling pickups or coupon redemptions with your consent.
  • Working with service providers like Firebase (bound by contracts).
  • Required by law or to protect safety/property.

Security

We use encryption, access controls, and other safeguards. However, no system is 100% secure, so avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request your data.
  • Correction: Fix inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data.
